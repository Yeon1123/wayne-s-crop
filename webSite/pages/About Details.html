About Details

We made an irrigation system with our sensor data and survey data. The introduction of this irrigation system is these.

First, we will gather temperature and humidity data with the sensor using the LoRa gateway. Also, you can see it each 15 seconds at the "Data Analysis" page. Compare temperature data and humidity data with accurate data. You can see it at the "Eco system" page. If humidity is low than accurate humidity data, The water pump open while 10 minutes. It stops for 1 day (24 hours) after operate. If you think farming is over, you can turn off the whole working at the "Settings" page. It can make farming effectively, even use waterless.

We want to compare the climate data, standard data(pour the water 20mm a week), and our irrigation system. We want to know is it statistically different. Also, what we want to know is that our irrigation system is eco-friendly (use less water) than standard farming. So, we made data with climate data(2010 ~ 2018).

We gather data for about 9 years(2010 ~ 2018)'s highest temperature(Celsius) and rainfall(mm), and predicting how many glasses of water is needed. The climate data is just rainfall data which is not pouring water. standard data seems if we pour the water 20mm a week, the sum of the rainfall and pouring water. Irrigate data seems if we pour the water with our irrigate system. If rainfall is too much, we don't pour the water, and if rainfall is too less, we pour the water much.
