LoRa/LoRaWAN Tutorial 1 : IoT, LPWAN, Semtech, LoRa

  1. What is IoT?
    IoT is a network of physical devices that are connected to the Internet and are able to “talk” to each other. Examples of the technology are as follows,
    • Short-range wireless communication
    • Cellular communication
    • LP WAN communication

  2. LP WAN(Low-Power Wide Area Network)
    Semtech Corporation developed LoRa.
    LoRa can send small data packages over the long distance. Cellular connection uses too much power, about 500mW. LoRa uses about 20mW, and has much better range than cellular. In the urban area, it might be anywhere from 2~5km(1.24~3.10 mile), however in rural areas, where they are not many obstructions, the range can be much greater. A negative aspect of Lora is that it can only send small amounts of data(0.3~0.5 kbps). Other negative aspects of Lora are its limitation to only transmit sensor data such as temperature, humidity, etc.

  3. LoRa UseCase
    • Smart Utilities(Water level monitoring, Utility meter, Fuel monitoring etc)
    • Health and Hygiene(Environmental monitoring, Waste management etc)
    • Safety(Smart lighting, Water level monitoring)
    • Agriculture(Monitoring animal welfare, Monitoring plant growing conditions)
